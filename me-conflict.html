ME Conflict Impact — India–EU/NA Routes

Middle East carrier traffic from India to North America & Europe collapsed in March 2026. QR down 80–84%, EY down 53–59%, EK down 39–43% across BLR/DEL/BOM.

Key finding — Mar 2026: ME carriers lost ~380K pax on India–NA/EU routes vs Mar 2025. BLR's via-ME share fell from 43.1% to 18.7% — a 24.4 point collapse in a single month. European and East Asian carriers (LH, VS, KL, CX) have absorbed significant share.
