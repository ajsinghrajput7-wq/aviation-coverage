Middle East conflict impact on India aviation — BLR vs DEL vs BOM, Mar 2026 analysis

ME Conflict Impact — Mar 2026: ME carrier traffic from India to NA+EU collapsed in March 2026. QR (Qatar) down 80–84% across BLR/DEL/BOM. EY (Etihad) down 53–59%. EK (Emirates) down 39–43%. Combined, ME carriers lost ~380K pax on India–NA/EU routes vs Mar 2025. European and East Asian carriers have absorbed significant share.
